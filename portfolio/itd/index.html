About Us

As the Global IT Sourcing Leader for xxx, Mr. xxx discovered just how challenging it is to grow diversity spend in the IT and Telecom Spend categories, since most technology companies and telecom carriers are not recognized as Diversity Suppliers. IT Diversity is changing that reality thru a portfolio of IT products and services that are delivered directly, thru agency agreements or as a value added reseller. From IT Infrastructure to Broadband to Staff Augmentation, IT Diversity is providing Corporations a path to optimal solutions, while supporting Diversity Spend.

Biography

I’ve had the good fortune of being intimately involved in the launch of Internet related technologies which have transformed the commercial economy. Beginning with the deployment of 80 of the earliest fiber broadband networks and the launch of the first competitive switched voice service in the 1990’s. Driving competitive broadband deployment at TCG and e.Spire, enhanced consumer choice and reduced the cost of high speed communications.
