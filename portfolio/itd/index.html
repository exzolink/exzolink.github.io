About Us

As the Global IT Sourcing Leader for a Fortune 500 Company, Mr. Martino discovered just how challenging it is to grow diversity spend in the IT and Telecom procurement categories. Most major technology companies and telecom carriers are publicly traded and not recognized as Diversity Suppliers.

IT Diversity is changing that reality thru a portfolio of IT products and services that are delivered directly, thru agency agreements or as a value added reseller. From IT Infrastructure to Broadband to Technology Expense Management and Staff Augmentation, IT Diversity is providing corporations a path to Diverse Solutions, Diverse Routes and Diverse Spend.

Biography

Nieves Iglesias

Nieves serves as the Chair and majority owner of IT Diversity and ensures that the firm’s mission is focused on creating opportunity and recognition for MBE\WBE suppliers within major enterprises. Nieves’ career has centered around executive roles designed to improve lives and provide opportunities to society’s most vulnerable members. She has been a successful advocate and professional fundraiser for education; raising funds for college scholarships for inner-city students in New Jersey, funding art programs at Hunter College in New York and leading the Development Department for a private school in North Carolina.
Nieves recognizes that, while IT and Technology have advanced our society, it has also created a digital divide. Her focus on providing access to quality technology and education for everyone is grounded in her own experiences and the opportunities a formal education has brought to her life. She holds a Masters Degree in Public Administration (MPA) from New York University and a BA in Political Science from The University at Albany.
Nieves was born in Spain, emigrated to the United States, has lived throughout the Atlantic Coast and is now delighted to call North Carolina her home. She has three amazing children and is married to her college sweetheart. Blessed to live on beautiful Lake Norman, her perfect day involves reading a good book, making someone smile and coaching a young person to envision a better life .

Biography

Jamie Martino

Jamie serves as the firm’s Chief Operating Officer and leads Business Development and Delivery. Over the last 30 years, Jamie has been intimately involved in the launch of Internet related technologies, which have transformed the commercial economy. His early career included the deployment of 80 of the earliest fiber broadband networks and the launch of the first competitive switched voice service. Driving competitive broadband deployment at TCG and e.Spire, Jamie enhanced consumer choice and reduced the cost of high speed communications for businesses.
Jamie has unique leadership experience in IT sourcing, procurement, and expense management. As CEO of Last Mile Connections and Avotus Corporation, he designed sourcing software solutions, which disrupted an industry and enhanced leverage for buyers. As the Global Leader of IT Sourcing at a Fortune 500 Company, he managed over $300M in IT Spend and achieved record results for Savings, Diversity Spend, Working Capital and Employee Engagement. As the Global Leader for Access Management at Global Crossing, he managed a $2.8 billion P&L and achieved savings of over $225M.
In addition to IT Diversity, Jamie is an active leader for Procurement Foundry, Leadership on the Lake, Charlotte SIM, LKN IT Professionals and the Cove Church. He enjoys wake surfing, golfing, tennis, and a home cooked meal with his wife, 3 children and an amazing group of friends with big appetites.
